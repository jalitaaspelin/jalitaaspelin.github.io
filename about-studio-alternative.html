Thoughtful and beautifully designed solutions

UI & Web Design

Hi! I'm Jalita and the designer behind Obiang. I love creating minimal beautiful solutions for engaging user stories. Passionate about typography and colour palettes, I craft UI elements and question UX journeys. I design websites, logos and visual identities.

Bitten with wanderlust, I have a background in Agile Project Management and Marketing with experience from Stockholm, London, Paris, Tokyo and Shanghai. I've worked with some of the best creative agencies in London and learned from outstanding art directors, creatives and developers to develop an eye for detail and perfection. Curious and fearless, my passion for design drives me forward to find the perfect composition.

With remote wings and roots in Sweden I love listening to Future Islands.

Drop me a line or say hello if you're in Sweden.
Your success is my success.
